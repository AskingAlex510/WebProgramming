Fees

For Store owners:

Selling fee

For store owners, every time an item sells, you will be charged a certain percentage based on the type of product from that purchase plus a 50 cent maintenence fee

Type/category of products Fees Notes
Most categories, including motors, parts, acessories and engines with capacity under 150CC
  • 12% on total sale amount upto 500$(apx 10,000,000 VND)
  • 2% on sale under 500$(apx 10,000,000 VND)
For products whose price needs to be negotiated when contacted, the fee will be 15% of the average price of the products in category
Books, Movies and Musics
  • 15% of total amount of sale upto 50$(apx 1,000,000 VND)
  • 6% of total sale over 50$(apx 1,000,000 VND)
Heavy industries's machineries and equipments including engines(over 150CC), metal presses, cranes, Cnc machines, industrial chemicals, ...
  • 5% of total sale upto 1500$(apx 30,000,000 VND)
  • 2% of items over 1500$(apx 30,000,000 VND)
 For engines with capacity under 150CC, check above
Computers, electronics and it's components
  • 12,5% of total sale amount upto 500$(apx 10,000,000 VND)
  • 8% total sale amount over 500$(apx 10,000,000 VND)
